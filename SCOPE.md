# Research Scope Definition
Template

## University
To be defined upon instantiation.

## Academic Unit
To be defined (faculty, department, or research group).

## Research Focus
This workspace may include research related to:
- audit and integrity models,
- transparency mechanisms,
- verification logic,
- ethical constraints in system design.

The exact focus must be defined before active research begins.

## Exclusions
This workspace must not include:
- production code,
- real-world data,
- security credentials,
- political or governmental decision logic.

## Time Frame
Research activities are expected to be:
- time-bound,
- semester-based or phase-based,
- explicitly documented.

## Deliverables
Expected outputs may include:
- academic reports,
- research papers,
- design evaluations,
- documented experimental results.
